About Us

Frequency (X) Labs builds consumer experiences for the world we're heading into, not the one we're leaving behind. As a fully integrated venture studio, we create and launch companies in-house, anticipating how human behavior and daily optimization will evolve in the coming years. Each venture is led by a specialized internal partner while drawing from our shared infrastructure, design thinking, and capital. We're not just building for today's market, we're engineering the behavioral tools and experiences that will become essential as society, work, and personal optimization continue to transform. This long-term perspective allows us to create genuinely valuable, lasting impact in the consumer landscape.
